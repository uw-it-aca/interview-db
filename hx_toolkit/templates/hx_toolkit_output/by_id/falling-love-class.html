Falling in Love with a Class

No matter your major (yes, some courses you just must take), choosing your classes continues to be a way to explore new ideas, challenge yourself, and be immersed in different worlds. You can make those NWs and VLPAs work for you!

Here are some ideas for using opportunities wisely:

  • Check out the current courses being taught faculty who have won Distinguished Teaching Awards.
  • Think back on your favorite class or subject â€“ what courses will give you the opportunity to dive deeper?
  • Is your favorite faculty teaching something new? Email them and ask!
  • Advisers have suggestions for great courses in their departments; searching websites and blogs can often unearth some gems.
  • Ask your friends about their favorite classes.
